Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Most projects range from 8-12 weeks from kickoff to deployment. This includes discovery, design, development, testing, and handoff phases.

What technologies do you use?

We use open standards (RDF, OWL, SHACL) and modern tools (Python, Kubernetes, cloud-native services). We avoid vendor lock-in and ensure you own your knowledge graph.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes. All projects include 30 days of post-launch support. We also offer retainer agreements for ongoing development and maintenance.

Can you work with our existing data infrastructure?

Absolutely. We integrate with your existing systems, databases, and data sources. Our pipelines are designed to work alongside your current infrastructure.

What's included in the handoff?

Complete documentation, source code, deployment guides, team training sessions, and access to all project artifacts. You own everything.
